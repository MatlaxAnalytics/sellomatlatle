Projects

Here you will find examples of my work and projects I have worked on. Advices and suggestions for future projects and improvements on projects below will be highly appreciated, Thank you!

Fatal Police Killings in the USA - A Maven Analytics Challenge

In this project I have visualised the fatal shootings of people by the police in the United States of America. This dataset was downloaded from The Washington Post @washingtonpost on August 16, 2022. Four (4) programs (Excel, SQL, Python and Power BI) were used throughout the project. The dataset was in excel csv format, cleaned in SQL and exported to excel file, explored in python, and the final product was visualised in Power BI.

Text Analysis of Junior Data Analyst Job Descriptions in SA

In this project I have analysed the job descriptions of vacant junior data analyst job Opportunities in South Africa. The dataset was scrapped in Python from indeed website using BeautifulSoup and Selenium, it was exported to an excel file, and later analysed using Python. Only two (2) programs (Python and Excel) were used throughout the project.

Business Analysis of Plato Pizza Sales - A Maven Analytics Challenge

In this project I have visualised the performance of the Plato Pizza Sales business. I have also provided recommendations and suggestions to improve the performance of the business. The dataset was downloaded from Maven Analytics website in csv format (4 csv files). The project was done using Only Power BI.
